Company Overview

TargetCo is a European technology firm specializing in non-toxic preservation systems for high-value bulk commodities. Established over a decade ago, the company has developed a proprietary, partially patent-defended approach to extending the shelf life of sensitive agricultural goods.

The core technology utilizes Controlled Atmosphere (CA) and Modified Atmosphere Packaging (MAP)—including specialized equipment for bulk packaging formats—to create ultra-low oxygen environments. This proven, chemical-free method offers a superior alternative to traditional fumigation.

  • Business Model: Operates on a "razor-and-blade" model with initial system sales and a predictable stream of re-occurring revenue from essential consumables.
  • Intellectual Property: Technology is partially defended by a portfolio of patents, creating a competitive moat.

Financials

TargetCo exhibits a robust financial profile with a growing base of re-occurring revenue from consumables. This indicates high customer retention and increasing utilization of the installed base of systems.

Key Market & Growth Drivers

TargetCo operates in the ~$16B Modified Atmosphere Packaging (MAP) market, supported by non-cyclical, structural tailwinds ensuring stable long-term growth (~7.9% CAGR).

  • Sustainability Mandate: Shift away from chemical fumigants towards non-toxic alternatives.
  • Food Waste Reduction: Critical technology for extending shelf life and preserving value.
  • Supply Chain Integrity: Advanced packaging needed to protect quality in global trade.

Investment Thesis

Acquire a profitable industrial technology asset with a defensible hardware niche and transform it into a high-growth, tech-enabled service provider with recurring sales. The strategy is to execute a three-pronged value creation plan:

Professionalize & Scale

Implement sophisticated sales and marketing processes to drive growth.

Tech-Enablement

Integrate "Smart" IoT sensor technology to create a premium, data-driven product offering.

HaaS Transition

Pivot the business model to recurring revenue, addressing the customer CapEx barrier.
