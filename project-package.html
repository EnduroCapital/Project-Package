Company Overview

TargetCo is a European technology firm specializing in non-toxic preservation systems for packaging of high-value bulk commodities. Established over a decade ago, the company has developed a proprietary, partially patent-defended approach to extending the shelf life of sensitive agricultural goods. The core technology utilizes Controlled Atmosphere (CA) and Modified Atmosphere Packaging (MAP) to create ultra-low oxygen environments by substituting atmospheric air with natural nitrogen.

This 100% environmentally friendly technique protects products from insects, toxins, mold, and moisture, offering a superior alternative to traditional fumigation. With a global footprint and projects completed worldwide, the company is perfectly positioned to capitalize on the increasing demand for food safety and waste reduction.

  • Business Model: Operates on a “razor-and-blade” model with initial system sales and a predictable stream of re-occurring revenue from essential consumables.
  • Intellectual Property: Technology is partially defended by a portfolio of patents, creating a competitive moat.

Financials

TargetCo exhibits a robust financial profile with a growing base of re-occurring revenue from consumables. This indicates high customer retention and increasing utilization of the installed base of systems.

Key Market & Growth Drivers

  • Sustainability Mandate: Shift away from chemical fumigants towards non-toxic alternatives.
